SERENG

The upcoming regional film *Sereng* is all set to release on 1st May 2026 across Jharkhand, promising a rich cultural experience for audiences. The movie will be released in four languages—Hindi, Nagpuri, Santhali, and Khortha—making it accessible to a diverse regional audience while celebrating local identity and storytelling. Featuring a talented cast including Vivek Nayak, Nitesh Kachhap, and Sweta Prajapati, the film is expected to bring strong performances to the screen. Directed by NPK, *Sereng* is already generating buzz among regional cinema lovers and is anticipated to make a significant impact upon its release. .

Artists unite to demand a strong film policy for the growth and recognition of Jharkhand’s regional cinema industry. ..

The Jharkhand Kalakar Andolan Sangharsh Samiti has been continuously working to put pressure on the state government to introduce a dedicated film policy aimed at strengthening the regional film industry in Jharkhand. The group, representing local artists and filmmakers, has been raising its voice through campaigns, discussions, and public appeals, emphasizing the urgent need for structured support, funding opportunities, and better infrastructure for regional cinema. Their efforts highlight how a well-defined policy could not only uplift local languages like Nagpuri, Santhali, and Khortha but also create employment, preserve cultural identity, and encourage new talent. As the movement gains momentum, it reflects the growing determination of Jharkhand’s creative community to secure recognition and sustainable growth for its film industry. .
